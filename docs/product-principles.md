# Product Principles

> The strategic north star for every product decision made in this prototype and in production.

---

## What Halosight Is

Halosight is an **AI-powered execution intelligence platform** for field and outside sales teams.

The one-sentence definition:
> **Halosight turns every field rep visit into structured customer intelligence — automatically captured, instantly in Salesforce, immediately visible to management.**

This is not a note-taking app. Not a transcription tool. Not a meeting summarizer. Not a CRM replacement. Those framings dramatically undervalue the platform and put it in competitive frames it cannot win.

---

## The Three Moments

Everything the product does lives inside three moments in the field rep workflow:

**Before the visit**
AI surfaces account history, prior commitments, and conversation context in under 30 seconds. The rep walks in prepared without touching the CRM.

**During / after the visit**
Rep records a voice debrief. AI instantly structures the interaction — commitments, next steps, orders, customer signals — and writes directly to Salesforce. The rep never types anything.

**For management**
Structured summaries land in Salesforce immediately. Managers get field visibility without requiring reps to do additional reporting work.

---

## Strategic Category

**Revenue Execution Intelligence — Field Sales**

Not conversation intelligence (that's Gong, Chorus — inside sales). Not generic AI productivity. Not CRM. The platform is purpose-built for the in-person, in-motion, outside sales workflow: reps driving between accounts, visiting warehouses, distribution facilities, and customer sites.

---

## Core Strategic Priorities (ranked)

1. **Rep adoption** — If reps don't use it, nothing else matters. Reduce friction relentlessly.
2. **Execution consistency** — Standardize what good field rep behavior looks like across territories.
3. **CRM reliability** — Make Salesforce data trustworthy without requiring reps to maintain it manually.
4. **Management visibility** — Give leaders documented evidence of field activity, not rep-filtered summaries.
5. **Execution intelligence** — Surface patterns, risks, and opportunities that would otherwise be invisible.

---

## What the App Must Feel Like

Fast. Intelligent. Lightweight. Trustworthy. Conversational. Operational.

More like: **an intelligent field copilot**
Not like: **a traditional CRM**

The experience should feel like it's working *for* the rep, not demanding work *from* the rep.

---

## What We Never Build

- Features that add steps without removing more steps
- AI outputs that feel like black boxes (no mystery AI behavior)
- Screens that feel like form-filling
- Anything that increases CRM discipline burden on reps
- Generic productivity features that don't serve the field sales workflow specifically

## Primary ICP

Organizations with **10–200+ field/outside sales reps** who:
- Visit customers in person daily (distribution, industrial, automotive aftermarket, construction)
- Use Salesforce as their CRM
- Struggle with inconsistent field rep documentation
- Have managers who lack real visibility into customer interactions
